Understanding Your Microsoft Platform Options

Microsoft offers different platforms for business applications. Here's how Dataverse and Power Apps compare to other Microsoft solutions.

Dataverse + Power Apps

What it is: Microsoft's low-code platform combining a cloud database (Dataverse) with app building tools (Power Apps).

Best for: Custom business applications, workflow automation, and solutions that need to grow with your business.

Cost: Pay-per-user licensing, typically $20-40 per user/month.

Microsoft Dynamics CE

What it is: Full-featured CRM platform with Sales, Customer Service, Marketing, and Field Service modules.

Best for: Large enterprises needing comprehensive CRM functionality out-of-the-box.

Cost: $95-160 per user/month depending on the module.

Microsoft Dynamics Customer Engagement (CE) - the full-featured CRM platform including Sales, Customer Service, Marketing, and Field Service modules

Custom Development

What it is: Building applications from scratch using traditional development methods.

Best for: Highly specialized requirements that can't be met by existing platforms.

Cost: High development costs, ongoing maintenance, and infrastructure management.
